About Underdogs House

The main target of this web-application is, to increase the
popularity of unknown or even badly known music artists.
The functionality includes user entertainment through music,
user communication with comments, user upload suggestion
...

All music we publish are free to use. The common rights you
will find under the details of the tracks.
